Accessibility Statement

Last updated June 2026

Sunset Travel Vacations is committed to making its website accessible to as many people as possible, including visitors who use assistive technologies. We want planning your next trip to be easy for everyone.

Our approach

We aim to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA. Steps we have taken include:

  • Semantic, structured HTML so screen readers can navigate the content
  • Descriptive alternative text on meaningful images
  • Keyboard-accessible navigation and controls
  • Readable color contrast between text and backgrounds
  • Respecting the "reduce motion" setting for visitors who prefer less animation
  • A responsive layout that works across phones, tablets, and computers

Known limitations

Some content provided by third parties may not fully meet these standards. We work to improve accessibility on an ongoing basis.

Feedback

If you have trouble using any part of this website, or you have a suggestion, please let us know and we will do our best to help and to fix the issue. You can also reach Sandee directly by phone or email to plan your trip with personal assistance.
